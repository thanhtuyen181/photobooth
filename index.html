Tina's Photobooth

How It Works

  • Choose 2 or 4 photos to start a session.
  • Pose during the countdown and let the camera snap each shot.
  • Drag stickers onto the canvas to decorate your strip.
  • Click on the sticker to move it around.
  • Choose "Add frame" to add a frame around your strip.
  • Click Download Photo to save your creation.
